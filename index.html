Who are we ?

humbleTechs is Malaysia's first and only Worker Cooperative of Programers, Designers, Marketeers and Creatives with a Passion for delivering the best possible value for your budget.

Our team regroups a wide variety of different profiles, from the fresh grade to the retired industry experts, but something links us together : The willingness to reduce our costs to offer you the best possible price on your project needs while never sacrificing on the quality of the results !

Why 'Budget-Friendly' ?

Are you from a big company with ever-growing yearly targets but no increase in budget ? Or are you simply a fresh player in the economy, with an awesome idea but limited funds ? Whichever the case come find us : We promise to deliver quality on a budget.

How do we keep your prices so low ?

By cuttng down our cost on facilities, fancy coffee, working remotely (100% Malaysian and West-European workforce), and keeping our team goal-focused, we can easily offer the best rate in the professional field. We also do not believe in inefficient management and long internal meetings. The only manager in humbleTechs is the Client !

Why trust us ?

We would like to see you again next time you may need our services. It is in our best interest to serve you to the best of our abilities, so that you will feel fully satisfied : A happy client might recommend us to three potential clients. An angry one will tell nine to stay away from us. We also offer a full refund on any contract we may not be able to fulfill (although it never happened yet).

Do we need to meet in person ?

It is preferable but not required. At the very least we insist on having a video conference during which we will ensure we have understood your problem fully. We may also start proposing a few solutions for you to review. If one of our agent has contacted you, he/she may also wish to be present to facilitate our discussion.

Our Charter : A promise from Us to You.

Our 'Charter of Honest Conduct' (CHC) is the internal law of our Cooperative. Failing to follow its simple rules is risking being expelled from the Cooperative and removed from working on any projects (It has not happened yet !).

Honesty to the Client

Long-term business relationship are the most fruitful ones, but they require you to trust us. This means we cannot ever risk your trust for the sake of quick business. If we cannot fulfill your contract, we will clearly tell you why and refuse any deposit from you. If some money has already been given, it will be fully refunded.

Honesty to the Team

For the sake of the Client and our well-being, we do not entertain in office politics or long-term quarrel. We fight very often (talented people rarely agree on everything from the start) and then we all go have dinner and plan the execution of our next project.

Quality First, Profit Last.

While money is important, we have found that the real, life-fulfilling motivation comes from taking pride in our work. Not a number on a bank book. Poor quality is not an option. Productive perfectionism is Encouraged. Accuracy and Frequency of communication with our Client is Paramount. We do not accept contracts we cannot fulfill for the sake of business.

Best Practices

Each of us must share, weekly, one thing he/she has learned about his/her field's 'best practice'. This is a simple way to guarantee we never stagnate so that our Client can keep trusting us in a rapidly changing World of Techs.

Responsible Maintenance

While we may or may not offer warranty on your project, we believe that we are responsible for your project even after it has ended. If we realize a mistake has been made, long after our deal is concluded, your project will appear in our watchlist and we will contact you to fix the error free of charge, with your permission.

Fairness in Dealings

We strongly believe in taking as much as we give, not more. This honesty guarantees that Clients come back to us, projects after projects. without ever risking to over-pay in a tech field where they do not know the price. We do not follow market prices : We charge for the work we do only.
